# HR Analytics Simulation Platform

### AI-Powered Workforce Decision Intelligence for Banking Institutions

---

## Executive Overview

The **HR Analytics Simulation Platform** is a structured decision-intelligence system designed to transform traditional HR analytics education into an interactive, strategy-driven simulation environment tailored for banking institutions.

Modern banks operate within complex ecosystems where workforce decisions directly impact financial performance, regulatory compliance, operational stability, and risk exposure. Despite this, most HR education remains descriptive and static.

This platform bridges that gap.

It integrates predictive attrition modeling, workforce optimization logic, gamified decision mechanics, and SaaS-based financial modeling into a unified simulation architecture.

The result is a scalable, decision-based learning and strategy environment that mirrors real-world banking workforce dynamics.

---

## Problem Statement

Traditional HR education relies on:

- Static dashboards
- Descriptive reporting
- Simplified case studies
- Limited exposure to predictive modeling
- No structured budget-tradeoff simulation

This leads to a capability gap between academic knowledge and real-world HR leadership decision-making.

The core question addressed by this project:

> How can HR professionals be trained to balance cost control, talent retention, compliance risk, and diversity mandates within realistic banking constraints?
>

## Solution Architecture

The platform is built across five integrated layers:

### 1️⃣ Data Layer

- Structured synthetic workforce dataset (100+ employees)
- Compensation, performance, engagement, compliance, and attrition variables
- Relational workforce modeling

### 2️⃣ Analytical Engine

- Correlation analysis
- Salary quartile segmentation
- Tenure cohort modeling
- Risk factor weighting

### 3️⃣ Predictive Flight Risk Model

Multi-factor weighted scoring logic based on:

- Tenure risk
- Compensation positioning
- Performance–promotion gap
- Manager effectiveness
- Engagement index
- Compliance status
- Departmental risk multiplier

Employees are classified into:

- Low Risk
- Moderate Risk
- High Risk
- Critical Risk

Designed for interpretability and business explainability.

### 4️⃣ Decision & Event Engine

Simulates:

- Retention bonus allocation
- Promotion approvals
- Hiring decisions
- Budget reallocation
- Compliance enforcement

Includes stochastic event modeling:

- Market salary inflation
- Regulatory audits
- Economic downturn
- Retirement waves

### 5️⃣ Scoring & Evaluation Framework

Composite score balancing:

- Strategic outcomes
- Financial discipline
- Workforce stability
- Compliance adherence
- Operational efficiency
